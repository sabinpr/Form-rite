How Do I Choose the Right Kitchen Benchtop Surface?

Porcelain

Highly Durable & Low Maintenance – Resistant to scratches, heat, and stains, with a non-porous surface that requires minimal upkeep.

Dekton

Ultra-Compact & Seamless – Extremely strong, resistant to heat, UV, and stains, with large slab sizes for a sleek, modern finish.

Engineered Stone (Quartz)

Easy to Maintain & Durable – Non-porous, stain-resistant, and scratch-resistant, making it ideal for busy kitchens.
